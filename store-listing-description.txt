WHAT IS QUERY FAN-OUT?
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Query fan-out is the technique AI search systems use to answer complex questions. Instead of running one search, the AI breaks your prompt into multiple sub-queries — related topics, implicit questions, comparisons, recency checks, and reformulations — then searches them all in parallel and synthesizes the results into a single answer.

Google AI Mode does this with Gemini. ChatGPT Search does it every time it browses the web. Claude does it when web search is enabled. Research shows a typical prompt fans out into 9–11 sub-queries, and deep research runs can trigger hundreds of searches from a single question.

Here's the problem: those fan-out queries are invisible. They never show up in Google Search Console, keyword tools report them as zero-volume, and no analytics platform captures them. Yet they decide which pages get retrieved, which brands get cited, and who wins AI visibility.

This extension makes them visible.
